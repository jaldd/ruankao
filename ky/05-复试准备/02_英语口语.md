# 英语口语

> 学习日期：2026-05-26 生成
> 阶段：初试后准备（初试前不用看）
> 分值占比：复试 10-15%
> 特点：不用完美，能交流就行

---

## 一、复试口语形式

| 环节 | 内容 | 时长 |
|------|------|------|
| 自我介绍 | 个人背景、学习/工作经历 | 2-3分钟 |
| 问答 | 针对自我介绍提问 | 3-5分钟 |
| 话题讨论 | 抽题讨论 | 3-5分钟 |

---

## 二、自我介绍模板

> Good morning/afternoon, professors. My name is ___. I am ___ years old. I graduated from ___ University with a degree in ___. After graduation, I have been working at ___ as ___ for ___ years.
>
> During my work, I found that ___（为什么想读MEM）. I believe that the MEM program at Tsinghua University will help me ___（目标）. I am particularly interested in ___（研究方向）.
>
> In my spare time, I enjoy ___（爱好）. I am a person who is ___（性格特点）.
>
> That's all about me. Thank you.

## 三、常见问答

### 1. Why do you want to study MEM?
> I want to study MEM because I believe it will help me develop systematic management thinking and improve my ability to solve complex engineering management problems. In my current work, I often face challenges that require both technical knowledge and management skills.

### 2. Why Tsinghua?
> Tsinghua University is one of the top universities in China with an excellent reputation in engineering and management. The MEM program here offers a comprehensive curriculum and distinguished faculty. I believe studying here will provide me with the best platform for my career development.

### 3. What is your career plan?
> After completing the MEM program, I plan to ___（短期目标）. In the long term, I hope to ___（长期目标）. I believe the knowledge and skills I gain from this program will be essential for achieving these goals.

### 4. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
> My greatest strength is my ability to learn quickly and adapt to new situations. I am also a good team player. As for weaknesses, I sometimes focus too much on details. I am working on improving my ability to see the bigger picture.

### 5. Can you describe a challenge you faced at work?
> In my previous project, we faced a significant challenge when ___（描述挑战）. I handled it by ___（解决方法）. Through this experience, I learned that ___（收获）.

## 四、话题讨论常用表达

| 功能 | 表达 |
|------|------|
| 表达观点 | In my opinion / I believe that / From my perspective |
| 举例说明 | For example / For instance / Take... for example |
| 补充观点 | In addition / Moreover / Furthermore |
| 转折 | However / On the other hand / Nevertheless |
| 总结 | In conclusion / To sum up / All in all |
| 同意 | I agree with you / That's a good point |
| 礼貌反对 | I see your point, but / I respect your opinion, however |

## 五、备考建议

1. **每天朗读 15 分钟**：读英文文章，练语感
2. **录音回听**：录下自己的回答，找问题
3. **模拟面试**：找朋友或用 AI 模拟
4. **不要背长文**：记关键词，用自己的话说
5. **放慢语速**：慢一点比结巴好
