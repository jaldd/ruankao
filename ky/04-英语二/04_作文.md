# 作文

> 学习日期：2026-05-26 生成
> 预计用时：3-4 小时学方法 + 每周练 2 篇
> 分值占比：25 分（小作文10分 + 大作文15分）
> 核心：模板 + 练习，作文是最容易提分的部分
> ⚠️ 英语作文是"背多分"，模板背熟就能拿 15+ 分

---

## 一、题型概述

### 小作文（应用文，10分）
- 100 词左右
- 类型：书信、通知、备忘录
- 常考：建议信、道歉信、邀请信、推荐信、感谢信、投诉信、辞职信、祝贺信

### 大作文（图表作文，15分）
- 150 词左右
- 类型：柱状图、饼图、折线图、表格
- 结构：描述图表 → 分析原因 → 总结展望

### 时间分配

| 部分 | 时间 |
|------|------|
| 小作文 | 15分钟 |
| 大作文 | 25分钟 |
| **总计** | **40分钟** |

## 二、小作文（应用文）

### 通用格式

```
Dear ___,

第一段：写信目的（1-2句）

第二段：具体内容（3-5句）

第三段：期待回复/表达祝愿（1-2句）

Yours sincerely,
Li Ming
```

### 各类书信模板

#### 1. 建议信

> Dear ___,
>
> I am writing to offer some suggestions regarding ___. As someone who ___, I would like to share my thoughts on this matter.
>
> First and foremost, it would be beneficial to ___. This is because ___. In addition, I suggest that ___. By doing so, ___.
>
> I hope you will find these suggestions helpful. I look forward to hearing from you.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

#### 2. 道歉信

> Dear ___,
>
> I am writing to express my sincere apology for ___. I deeply regret that ___.
>
> The reason for this is that ___. I understand that this may have caused you ___. To make up for it, I would like to ___.
>
> Once again, I am truly sorry for any inconvenience caused. I assure you that ___.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

#### 3. 邀请信

> Dear ___,
>
> It is my great honor to invite you to ___. The event will be held on ___ at ___.
>
> As ___, your presence would be highly appreciated. During the event, we will ___. I believe that your participation will ___.
>
> Please let me know if you can attend. I look forward to your reply.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

#### 4. 推荐信

> Dear ___,
>
> I am writing to recommend ___ for ___. I have known him/her for ___ years and can confidently say that ___.
>
> First, he/she is ___. For example, ___. Second, he/she has ___. This is evident from ___.
>
> I strongly recommend ___ without reservation. Please feel free to contact me if you need further information.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

#### 5. 感谢信

> Dear ___,
>
> I am writing to express my heartfelt gratitude for ___. Your ___ has been invaluable to me.
>
> Thanks to your help, I was able to ___. I particularly appreciate ___. This experience has ___.
>
> Thank you once again for your kindness and support. I hope to have the opportunity to return the favor.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

#### 6. 投诉信

> Dear ___,
>
> I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with ___. I purchased ___ on ___ and unfortunately, ___.
>
> Specifically, ___. This has caused me significant inconvenience. I would like to request that ___.
>
> I hope this matter can be resolved promptly. I look forward to your response.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

#### 7. 辞职信

> Dear ___,
>
> I am writing to inform you of my decision to resign from my position as ___, effective ___.
>
> I have appreciated the opportunity to work at ___ and have learned a great deal during my time here. However, ___.
>
> I will do my best to ensure a smooth transition. Thank you for your understanding.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

#### 8. 祝贺信

> Dear ___,
>
> I was thrilled to learn that ___. Please accept my warmest congratulations on this remarkable achievement.
>
> Your success is well-deserved, as you have always ___. I am confident that you will continue to excel in ___.
>
> Once again, congratulations! I wish you all the best in your future endeavors.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

## 三、大作文（图表作文）

### 写作结构（三段式）

```
第一段：描述图表（40-50词）
第二段：分析原因（70-80词）
第三段：总结展望（30-40词）
```

### 第一段：描述图表

**模板**：

> As is clearly illustrated in the chart/graph, ___（主要趋势）. Specifically, ___（数据1）, while ___（数据2）. It is obvious that ___（总体趋势）.

**常用表达**：

| 功能 | 表达 |
|------|------|
| 上升 | increase, rise, grow, climb, surge |
| 下降 | decrease, decline, fall, drop, slump |
| 持平 | remain stable, level off, stay constant |
| 占比 | account for, make up, constitute, represent |
| 对比 | compared with, in contrast to, whereas |
| 显著地 | significantly, dramatically, remarkably, substantially |
| 缓慢地 | gradually, slightly, modestly, marginally |
| 波动 | fluctuate, oscillate |

**描述句型**：

| 类型 | 句型 |
|------|------|
| 描述趋势 | The number of ___ increased/decreased from ___ to ___ |
| 描述占比 | ___ accounts for ___% of the total |
| 描述对比 | A is ___ times as much as B |
| 描述变化 | There was a significant increase/decrease in ___ |
| 描述极值 | ___ reached its peak/highest point at ___ |
| 描述转折 | However, ___ experienced a decline after ___ |

### 第二段：分析原因

**模板**：

> There are several reasons contributing to this phenomenon. First and foremost, ___（原因1）. With the development of ___, ___（展开说明）. Moreover, ___（原因2）. As ___, ___（展开说明）. Last but not least, ___（原因3）.

**常用原因角度**：

| 角度 | 可用表达 |
|------|----------|
| 经济发展 | economic growth, rising income, improved living standards |
| 技术进步 | technological advancement, internet, mobile technology |
| 政策推动 | government policy, regulation, incentive program |
| 观念变化 | changing attitudes, growing awareness, shifting values |
| 社会因素 | urbanization, aging population, globalization |
| 教育因素 | improved education, better access to information |

**原因分析句型**：

| 类型 | 句型 |
|------|------|
| 引出原因 | There are several reasons contributing to this phenomenon |
| 第一原因 | First and foremost, ___ / The primary reason is that ___ |
| 第二原因 | Moreover, ___ / In addition, ___ / Furthermore, ___ |
| 第三原因 | Last but not least, ___ / Finally, ___ |
| 因果关系 | This is because ___ / As a result, ___ |

### 第三段：总结展望

**模板**：

> In conclusion, ___（总结趋势）. Looking ahead, I believe this trend will continue in the foreseeable future. It is advisable that ___（建议）.

**常用结尾句型**：

| 类型 | 句型 |
|------|------|
| 总结 | In conclusion / To sum up / Overall / All in all |
| 预测 | This trend is likely to continue / I believe this trend will persist |
| 建议 | It is advisable that / Measures should be taken to |
| 积极 | This is a positive development / This trend is encouraging |
| 中性 | The situation deserves further attention |

## 四、大作文范文

### 范文 1：柱状图——大学生兼职比例变化

> As is clearly shown in the bar chart, the percentage of college students taking part-time jobs has experienced a significant increase from 35% in 2015 to 68% in 2023. This upward trend indicates that more and more students are choosing to work while studying.

> There are several reasons behind this phenomenon. First and foremost, the rising cost of education has compelled many students to seek part-time employment to ease their financial burden. Moreover, students are increasingly aware of the importance of practical experience. By working part-time, they can develop essential skills such as communication and time management, which will benefit their future careers. Last but not least, the growth of the gig economy has provided more flexible job opportunities that are compatible with academic schedules.

> In conclusion, the growing trend of college students taking part-time jobs reflects both economic necessity and a desire for practical experience. I believe this trend will continue as the job market becomes increasingly competitive. However, students should strike a balance between work and study.

---

### 范文 2：饼图——居民消费结构变化

> As is clearly illustrated in the pie chart, there have been notable changes in the consumption structure of Chinese residents between 2015 and 2023. Specifically, the proportion of spending on food decreased from 35% to 25%, while expenditure on education and entertainment rose from 15% to 25%. Meanwhile, spending on healthcare increased from 10% to 15%.

> Several factors contribute to this shift. First and foremost, the continuous growth of the economy has led to higher disposable income, enabling people to spend more on non-essential goods and services. Moreover, with the improvement of living standards, people are paying more attention to the quality of life rather than merely satisfying basic needs. The growing emphasis on children's education and personal development has also driven the increase in education-related spending. Last but not least, the aging population has contributed to the rise in healthcare expenditure.

> In conclusion, the changing consumption structure reflects the improvement of living standards and the shift in people's priorities. I believe this trend will continue as the economy develops further, and it is a positive sign of social progress.

---

### 范文 3：折线图——在线教育用户增长

> As is clearly depicted in the line graph, the number of online education users in China has witnessed a dramatic increase from 150 million in 2018 to 450 million in 2023. The growth was particularly sharp between 2020 and 2021, when the number nearly doubled.

> This remarkable growth can be attributed to several factors. First and foremost, the COVID-19 pandemic forced schools and training institutions to move their classes online, which greatly accelerated the adoption of online education. Moreover, the rapid development of internet technology and mobile devices has made online learning more accessible and convenient than ever before. People can now take courses anytime and anywhere using their smartphones. Last but not least, the increasing demand for lifelong learning and professional development has driven more adults to seek online education opportunities.

> In conclusion, the surge in online education users reflects both technological advancement and changing learning habits. I believe online education will continue to grow and complement traditional classroom learning. However, it is important to ensure the quality of online courses and bridge the digital divide.

---

### 范文 4：表格——不同年龄段健身方式偏好

> As is clearly presented in the table, people of different age groups have varying preferences for fitness activities. Among young people aged 18-30, running (45%) and gym workouts (35%) are the most popular choices. Middle-aged people (31-50) prefer walking (40%) and swimming (30%), while elderly people (above 50) mainly choose walking (55%) and Tai Chi (25%).

> The differences in fitness preferences can be explained by several factors. First and foremost, physical conditions vary across age groups. Young people generally have more energy and physical strength, which enables them to engage in high-intensity activities like running and gym training. Moreover, lifestyle differences play a significant role. Young people are often influenced by social media and fitness culture, while older adults tend to prefer gentler, low-impact exercises that are easier on the joints. Last but not least, time availability differs — young people may prefer efficient workouts, while retirees have more time for leisurely activities.

> In conclusion, the varying fitness preferences across age groups reflect different physical needs and lifestyles. It is advisable that fitness facilities offer diverse programs to cater to different age groups, and that individuals choose exercises suitable for their physical conditions.

## 七、常见语法错误（必须避免）

| 错误类型 | 错误示例 | 正确写法 |
|----------|----------|----------|
| 主谓一致 | The number of students are... | The number of students is... |
| 时态错误 | Last year he goes... | Last year he went... |
| 冠词遗漏 | I am student | I am a student |
| 中式英语 | open the light | turn on the light |
| 拼写错误 | enviroment | environment |
| 名词单复数 | many student | many students |
| 动词形式 | He enjoy reading | He enjoys reading |
| 介词错误 | in Monday | on Monday |
| 比较级错误 | more better | much better |
| 代词一致 | Everyone should do their best | Everyone should do his or her best |

## 八、作文提分技巧

### 技巧 1：用高级词汇替换简单词

| 简单词 | 高级替换 |
|--------|----------|
| good | beneficial, favorable, advantageous |
| bad | detrimental, adverse, harmful |
| important | crucial, significant, vital |
| many | numerous, a multitude of, a vast number of |
| think | believe, maintain, hold the view that |
| show | illustrate, demonstrate, reveal |
| because | due to, owing to, on account of |
| so | therefore, consequently, as a result |
| about | regarding, concerning, with respect to |
| help | facilitate, assist, contribute to |

### 技巧 2：用复合句替换简单句

| 简单句 | 复合句 |
|--------|--------|
| The economy grows. People live better. | As the economy grows, people enjoy a better life. |
| Technology develops. Work becomes easier. | With the development of technology, work has become much easier. |
| He studied hard. He passed the exam. | Thanks to his hard work, he passed the exam. |

### 技巧 3：避免重复用词

| 重复 | 替换 |
|------|------|
| increase...increase...increase | increase...rise...grow |
| many...many...many | many...numerous...a large number of |
| important...important...important | important...crucial...significant |

## 十一、本节核心结论（必背）

1. **小作文**：背 8 类书信模板，格式不能错
2. **大作文**：三段式——描述、分析、总结
3. **描述图表**：用具体数据说话，不能笼统
4. **分析原因**：2-3 个原因，从经济/技术/社会角度
5. **避免低级错误**：主谓一致、时态、拼写
6. **字数控制**：小作文 100 词，大作文 150 词
7. **每周至少练 2 篇**，限时完成
8. **高级词汇替换**简单词，提升语言档次
9. **复合句替换**简单句，增加句子复杂度
10. **4 篇范文**要熟读，4 篇练习要动手写
